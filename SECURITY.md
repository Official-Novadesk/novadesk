## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of Novadesk seriously. If you have discovered a security vulnerability in this project, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

**Please do not open a public issue.** Publicly reporting a vulnerability can put the community at risk. instead, please follow these steps:

1.  **Email Us**: Send a detailed description of the vulnerability to [officialnovadesk@gmail.com](mailto:officialnovadesk@gmail.com).
2.  **Include Details**: Please include as much information as possible to help us reproduce the issue (e.g., code snippets, reproduction steps, screenshots).
3.  **Wait for Response**: We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and provide an estimated timeline for the fix.

We will treat your report with strict confidentiality and credit you for your discovery (unless you wish to remain anonymous) once the vulnerability has been patched.

Thank you for helping keep Novadesk safe!
